    Redesign crypto transaction flow investigation expereince

    // 1 month | Product Design Design

    // 2 Designers + 1 PM + 3 Front-End Engineers + 1 backend engineer

    Intro

    User driven approach: A transaction visualization tools that help compliance professionals and OTC owners identify crypto crime and risks

    1. Use scenarios & User journey

    Target users

    Define a shared design pattern that fit into three different journeys: portfolio management tool, financial advisor and customers.

    Use scenarios

    • When compliance team get alerts from system(e.g. a large transaction happens between an address and a crime address), to further investigate who else and how much fund are involved in the flow, they will come to the graph and analyse here.

    • The process involve large data, complicated interactions, high-frequency update (every 40-60 min)

    • Device: small-screen laptop and tablet

    Goal

    After completing the analysis and information synthesis, save the graph and document it locally.
